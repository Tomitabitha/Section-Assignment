In addition to layout and format, CSS is responsible for font color and more.

Difference between paddings, margins, and borders

Padding is the inner space between the content and the border of your box.

Border is known as the perimeter of the box, a border goes around the padding and content
Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line like the green one pictured above.

Margin is the outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box. Margin Clears an area outside the border, the margin is transparent.
